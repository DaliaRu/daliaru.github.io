Branding

JONAVA CITY

I.

Project

The project of Jonava city municipality, in cooperation with Kaunas University, to create a brand for the city of Jonava.

II.

Concept

In the created trademark, the main emphasis of the Jonava city trademark is the contour of the Neris river. Jonava city is on the coast of Neris, which is characterized by a sharp relief and the river is well known as the city of Jonava founding symbol. The flow of the river symbolizes the past and time and constant renewal. The trademark of the city of Jonava is highlighted letter O - this represents the Jonava ring road. These are state-owned value paths. The geographical location of Jonava is convenient for Lithuania and international connections.
